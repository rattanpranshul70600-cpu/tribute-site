Tribute to Nicola Tesla

The Man Who Invented the Twentieth Century

1. Full Name & Birth

Nicola Tesla was born on July 10, 1856, in Smiljan (modern-day Croatia). He was of Serbian ethnicity.

2. Electrical Engineering Genius

He was a brilliant inventor, electrical engineer, and futurist who made major contributions to modern electricity systems.

3. Alternating Current (AC) System

Tesla developed the Alternating Current (AC) electrical system, which is still used worldwide today to power homes and cities.

4. The “War of Currents”

Tesla worked with George Westinghouse. to promote AC electricity, competing against Thomas Edison, who supported Direct Current (DC).

5. Tesla Coil

He invented the Tesla Coil in 1891, a device used to produce high-voltage electricity and still used in radio technology experiments.

6. Wireless Energy Vision

Tesla dreamed of transmitting electricity wirelessly across the globe through his project called Wardenclyffe Tower.

7. Radio Technology

Although radio is often credited to Guglielmo Marconi, Tesla’s earlier patents played a major role in radio communication technology.
